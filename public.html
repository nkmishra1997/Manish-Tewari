Public Services

Manish Tewari hails from a prominent political family that has been in public service in Punjab through the generations. His grand father Late Sardar Tirath Singh was a Senior Advocate in the Punjab & Haryana High Court, a Freedom fighter and a Minister in the Congress Govt. of Punjab and the erstwhile State of PEPSU 1950-1977. His father late Dr. V.N. Tewari was a Professor of Comparative Modern Indian Literature at Punjab University, Chandigarh and a nominated Member of Parliament (Rajya Sabha). He was assassinated by the terrorists in 1984. His late mother, Dr. (Mrs) Amrit Tewari is a Professor Emeritus and Former Dean of the Post Graduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) Chandigarh and Former President of the Indian Dental Association and Dental Council of India.
